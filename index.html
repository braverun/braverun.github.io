What I Do

Board Game Bookshelf

Board Game Bookshelf was my final project at the Iron Yard. The app is a simple way to keep track of your board game collection. BGB utilizes a Ruby backend that I built using the lightweight framework, Sinatra. The meat of the app is Ember.js. I also utilized SASS for styling and Parse for an additional backend.
